## Structure

This page is divided into two main sections:

1. **`Explore Watchlists`**

   These are specific asset lists curated by the Santiment Team based on category.

2. **`My Watchlists`**

   These are the user's custom watchlists.

![](page.png)

## Available Categories 

- **`All Assets`**: All tokens we track.
- **`DeFi`**: Decentralized finance related tokens.
- **`ERC 20`**: Exclusively displays Ethereum-based ERC20 tokens.
- **`NFT`**: List of NFT related tokens, not just NFTs themselves.
- **`Stablecoins`**: Assets that are intended to maintain a fixed value. 
- **`Trending Assets`**: A dynamic category showing assets that are showing up in our tending words, stories, and narrative tools. 

## How to Create a Watchlist

1. Click the "Create watchist" button
2. Name it and decide whether it will be private or public, then hit create.
3. Select the assets.
