# Introduction

Santiment delivers real-time, battle-tested cryptocurrency market intelligence through our features and metrics for on-chain data, social sentiment, developer activity, market behavior, and more. By connecting Santiment's rich dataset directly to modern AI tools, you can build smarter agents, automate deep market research, generate actionable insights, and power next-level trading or analytics workflows. In this section you’ll learn how to quickly get up and running with our official MCP server for seamless integration with Claude Desktop, Claude Code, and any MCP-compatible agent, our custom Claude Skill, plus our GraphQL & REST APIs and additional AI-ready tools. Whether you’re prototyping an autonomous research assistant or production-grade crypto intelligence agents, this guide will give you a good idea about what's possible and how.
